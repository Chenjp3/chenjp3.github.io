## Research Focus

My research focuses on the progenitors, physical mechanisms, and observational properties of high-energy transient phenomena. By combining theoretical modeling, multi-wavelength data analysis, and hydrodynamic simulations, I study the dynamical evolution and radiation processes of transient sources.
